Card Diary

Minimalists Micro-Diarying App

Card Diary is a perfect place for you to think, feel, discover, expand, remember, and dream. The age we're living in is overloaded with information. Sometimes it gets so noisy it's hard to hear your own voice. Journaling is all about taking a break, turning your attention inwards and listen to what is going on in your mind.

Card Diary is a beautifully designed micro-diarying app for capturing and saving your thoughts, ideas or memories. Your space for retrospecting and observing the contents of your mind.

Card Diary is designed to be clean and minimalistic to bring focus on the words you fill it with.

If you journal on a daily basis, going back to your previous messages and navigating through and searching your entries is just a few taps away with Card Diary powerful history tool.
